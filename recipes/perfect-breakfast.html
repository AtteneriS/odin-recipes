The perfect breakfast recipe

What's the perfect breakfast? A delicious combination of unique favors my mom put together by accident! I hope others will enjoy it as much as we do.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  • Dijon mustard
  • Â½ avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add eggs and cook until whites are mostly firm.
  3. Break yolks and continue cooking until eggs are set and no longer runny, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  4. Spread one side of toasted sourdough with Dijon mustard.
  5. Arrange avocado slices on bread and top with cooked eggs. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
